# Bitcoin Bottom Index Dashboard

**Live site:** https://brls123.github.io/BBI-Page/

Bitcoin Bottom Index (BBI) is a Bitcoin market dashboard focused on identifying bottom-friendly conditions, risk regimes, and recovery structure.

## What this page shows

The dashboard is designed to answer a simple question:

**Is Bitcoin currently in a bottoming zone, a repair phase, a trend bull phase, or a risk-off environment?**

To do that, the page combines several layers of information into one view:

- a composite BBI score
- regime classification
- macro / technical / fundamental dimensions
- US equities and altcoin breadth context
- historical analogs and scenario comparisons

## What is BBI?

BBI stands for **Bitcoin Bottom Index**.

It is a composite index built to estimate how favorable current conditions are for Bitcoin accumulation and bottom formation. It is not a price prediction model and it is not meant to call exact tops or bottoms tick by tick. Instead, it helps frame whether the broader environment looks:

- deeply discounted
- improving from stress
- neutral / mixed
- extended or risk-heavy

## How BBI is built

The index combines multiple categories of signals rather than relying on one metric alone.

### 1. Valuation
These indicators try to measure whether Bitcoin is cheap or expensive relative to historical norms.

Examples shown on the page include:

- **AHR999**
- **Mayer Multiple**

### 2. Sentiment
Sentiment helps show whether the market is in fear, panic, neutrality, or greed.

Example:

- **Fear & Greed Index**

### 3. Macro environment
Macro conditions matter because Bitcoin does not trade in isolation. Liquidity, rates, dollar strength, and broader risk appetite can all affect whether bottoms are likely to hold.

### 4. Cycle / technical structure
This layer looks at whether Bitcoin is breaking down, stabilizing, repairing, or moving back into trend alignment.

### 5. Onchain context
Onchain data helps indicate whether long-term holder behavior and broader network conditions are supportive, mixed, or weak.

### 6. Cross-market context
The dashboard also includes external context, especially:

- **US equities / risk appetite**
- **Altcoin breadth and BTC dominance**

This matters because some environments support BTC-led recovery, while others favor broad alt participation or broad risk reduction.

## Regimes shown on the dashboard

The page maps current conditions into one of several market regimes:

- **Deleveraging** — stress, weak breadth, and higher downside risk
- **BTC Repair** — Bitcoin stabilizes first while broader participation is still limited
- **BTC Bull** — stronger trend alignment and healthier risk appetite
- **Alt Season** — broader rotation beyond BTC with improving participation

These regimes are used to make the dashboard easier to interpret than a raw list of indicators.

## What the page is useful for

This dashboard is useful for people who want a structured way to think about Bitcoin market conditions, especially:

- whether current conditions are bottom-friendly
- whether BTC is repairing or trending
- whether alts are participating or still weak
- whether macro and equity conditions are helping or hurting
- how current conditions compare with historical analogs

## Important note

BBI is an analytical framework, not financial advice. It is intended to improve market context and decision quality, not to guarantee outcomes.
